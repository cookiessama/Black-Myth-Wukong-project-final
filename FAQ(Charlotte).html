Frequently asked questions

  • Is Black Myth: Wukong an open-world game?
    Black Myth: Wukong is not an open world game. However, there is plenty of variety in the fairly linear game structure. The game has an open end composition, allowing some diversity.
  • How long does it take to finish Black Myth: Wukong?
    It takes about 40 hours to complete Black Myth: Wukong, while completing most side quests, as well as the main story. If you want to collect all the trophies and attain all achievements, it can take around 70 hours to finish the game.
  • Can I play Black Myth: Wukong on Xbox?
    Unfortunately, Black Myth: Wukong is not available on Xbox. Players need a PC or an PS5 to access the game.
  • Can I play Black Myth: Wukong on PS Plus?
    No, Black Myth: Wukong is not available for PS Plus users. You would need a PS5 to purchase the game.
  • What is the recommended age to start playing Black Myth: Wukong?
    According to IMDb, Black Myth Wukong is rated the following:

    • ESRB: M
    • PEGI: 16
    • AUS: MA15+
    • USK: 18
    • CHI: 17
    • TAI: (Banned)
  • Can I play Black Myth: Wukong with multiple players?
    No, Black Myth: Wukong does not have any multiplayer features, co-op or competitive attributes.
  • How many trophies can you get in Black Myth: Wukong?
    For the PS5, there are a total of 36 trophies one can get when playing Black Myth: Wukong. The PC version has 81 achievements overall.
  • What is the Black Myth: Wukong compatibility mode?
    Players who access Black Myth: Wukong on their PC, have the option to enable the "Compatibility mode", before launching the game. This function disables certain features, to allow a better gaming experience. THese features include third-party programs like screen recorders, performance capture software, and more.
  • Does Black Myth: Wukong have difficulty settings?
    Black Myth: Wukong does not have any difficulty settings. Unfortunately, the development team said that there are no plans to add any.
  • How many different ending does Black Myth: Wukong have?
    Black Myth: Wukong actually has two endings. There is one normal ending one reaches, when finishing the game. The second, secret ending, can only be achieved by completing the game a second time.
