Gameplay

Black Myth: Wukong breaks away from traditional action games by removing the familiar mini-map and quest log. This is a world where discovery is key. Without hand-holding, you’ll need to rely on your instincts to uncover hidden secrets and progress. Carefully explore your surroundings, harness new skills, and unlock your true potential as you journey through the land.

Travel the land and sea

Explore the unknown

A true traveler navigates without a map. Let the glowing embers scattered across the landscape guide you on your path, leading you to new challenges and untold discoveries.

Unveil Hidden Secrets

Black Myth: Wukong is filled with secret areas that aren’t immediately obvious. Your keen eye and thorough exploration will reward you with valuable treasures and secrets, enhancing your adventure and strengthening your resolve.

Become a fight expert

Master powerful spells

Harness the magic of the world with a variety of powerful spells at your disposal. Whether you seek the mystical arts, the power of alteration, or the strategic advantage of strand spells, each spell offers unique abilities to turn the tide of battle in your favor.

Master the art of dodging

Engage in fast-paced, tactical combat where timing is everything. Dodge enemy attacks with precision to avoid devastating blows, and master the art of evasion to create mirror copies of yourself while slowing time for a brief moment. Perfect your dodging skills and gain the upper hand in combat, turning the flow of battle to your advantage.

Master the three stances of combat

Adapt to every situation with three distinct stances, each offering unique combat benefits. Choose wisely to outmaneuver your enemies and unleash devastating attacks: Pillar Stance, Smash Stance, Thrust Stance, what will it be?
