About the Game

"Black Myth: Wukong" is an action role-playing game inspired by the Chinese classic novel "Journey to the West." Players will take on the role of Sun Wukong, using various transformation abilities, combat skills, and mystical weapons to defeat enemies.

The goal of the game is to overcome various challenges through puzzle-solving and combat, unveiling the mysteries of the story.

Game Genre and Concept

Genre: Action Role-Playing

The game combines action elements with traditional Chinese narrative, blending stunning visual effects to provide an exciting combat experience. The overall concept is to offer players a fantasy adventure rooted in Chinese culture, exploring the mysteries and challenges of the "Journey to the West" world.
